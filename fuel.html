Why Conserve Fossil Fuels?

Fossil fuels such as coal, oil, and natural gas are non-renewable resources. Conserving them helps protect our environment, reduce pollution, and ensure sustainable energy for future generations.

Key Reasons to Conserve:

  • Reduce greenhouse gas emissions
  • Mitigate climate change
  • Preserve natural resources
  • Improve public health

Simple Ways to Conserve Fossil Fuels

  • Use public transportation, bike, or walk when possible
  • Switch to energy-efficient appliances and LED lighting
  • Turn off lights and electronics when not in use
  • Insulate your home to reduce heating and cooling needs
  • Support and use renewable energy sources
